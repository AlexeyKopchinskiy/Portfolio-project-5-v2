What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your actions and preferences over time.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic site functionality, such as logging in and navigating pages.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to personalize ads and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

Your Choices
