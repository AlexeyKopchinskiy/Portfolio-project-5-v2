🧾 Reviewer Guidelines

This page is accessible only to users in the Reviewer or Admin groups.

🔍 Review Criteria

  • Ensure the post aligns with editorial standards and tone.
  • Check for factual accuracy and proper citations.
  • Verify that images and media are appropriate and properly licensed.
  • Confirm that the post is free of grammatical and spelling errors.

🛠️ Reviewer Actions

  • Use the Reviewer Notes field to provide constructive feedback.
  • Set the Review Status to Reviewed, Needs Changes, or Draft.
  • Toggle Is Published only when the post is ready for public view.

📌 Best Practices

  • Be respectful and supportive in your feedback.
  • Communicate clearly with authors if major revisions are needed.
  • Avoid editing the post content directly unless absolutely necessary.
