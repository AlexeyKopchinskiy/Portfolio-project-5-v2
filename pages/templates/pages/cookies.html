1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information and personalized experiences.

2. Why We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They include things like login authentication and security.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our site, allowing us to improve usability and content.
  • Functionality Cookies: These remember your preferences, such as language or region, to enhance your experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use tools like Google Analytics to gather anonymous usage data and trends.
  • Marketing Cookies: These may be used to deliver relevant ads or track engagement with our promotional content.

3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can set your browser to refuse all or some cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of our site.
